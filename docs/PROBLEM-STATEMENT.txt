PROBLEM STATEMENT                                                                                        

  1.Energy cost is one of the largest operating expenses (OPEX) for telecom operators                      
                                                                                                           
  2.Cell-level energy-saving schemes are typically applied for maximum gain                                
                                                                                                           
  3.The current cell-level energy-saving mechanism

  •The Operations & Maintenance (OAM) detects a lowly loaded cell (in terms of PRB usage and the       
  number of connected users) and shuts it down

  •The operator may do it manually or set a timer for periodic shutdown

  4.Drawbacks of the current mechanism:

  •It relies on the current traffic load without considering future traffic load or other factors      

  •May result in deterioration of Energy Efficiency in the network because the future load is not      
  considered
